Online Bidder Enrollment


  1. How much does it cost to enroll online ?

    2. Online enrollment is free of cost.

  2. What is the validity of account after registration ?

    3. The account registered does not expire, it remains valid, however upon expiry of the DSC mapped, system will prompt to map new DSC.


  3. How do I log on to eAuction system after I have enrolled online ?

    4. Users can login by clicking on Login button, then mention your Login ID and Password along with Captcha and click on Submit, then login using Digital Signature.


  4. Is it possible to edit my profile details entered during online enrollment ?

    5. Yes, Profile information can be updated except Login ID and Company Name, rest of the information can be updated after logging into the account ->My Accounts-> Edit Profile option.
